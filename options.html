Domains
Add domains or text you'd like to match on separated by a new line.
Only the domains specified will be de-duplicated.
For example 'mail.google.com' or 'calendar.google.com' will treat gmail and calendar as singletons.
You can specify a specific string in a url excludes it from being treated as a duplicate by adding it in square brackets as a comma separated list.
For example 'mail.google.com[&view=pt&,&view=btop&]' will exclude email printing and pop out windows in gmail from being closed.
